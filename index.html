Features of Our Services

  • Eco-friendly and fish safe materials

    The materials we use in setting up homes for your lovely pets have been tested for any materials which can have negative effects on our ecosystem and have been approved by over 3 international companies worldwide. Also, over 75% of our goods are from recycled materials.

  • Affordable

    We aim to provide you the best quality and comfort for you and your little sea pets with as little expense as possible.

  • Fast Shipping

    We strive to ship the products as soon as they're ready to prevent any delay or discomfort to you and your pets. We also have well established and monitored systems to ensure your packages reach you in sound condition.

  • 24/7 Customer Support

    Our customer care service is always available, any time and anyday all week. Just place a call :)

  • Free Plushy!

    Aand for a limited time range, when you order a package from us, you recieve an AdorableFish plushy. This is your chance to show off your being a fishkeeper in tush all on us! :)
